Instructions

Objective
Embark on an epic journey as a young ninja to unlock the power of the ancient Orb. Complete quests, defeat enemies, and progress through different maps to reach the final challenge.

Controls
WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character.
Interact Key (if applicable): Use this to interact with objects or talk to characters.

Gameplay

Finding Objects: Look for hidden items or specific objects.
Counting Challenges: Find and count particular items or enemies.
Fight Scenes: Engage in battles along the way; be strategic to win!
Cutscenes: Reveal key story moments and hints about your destiny.


Time Limit
Each level has a time limit, so manage your time wisely. Tips will appear the longer youâ€™re on a quest to help guide you forward.

End Goal
Complete all quests, uncover the secrets of the elemental powers, and defeat the final challenge to finish the game!

Note
The game is designed for keyboard controls and is not mobile-friendly. For the best experience, play on a desktop or laptop.
